About Us

We are a small company with a passion for software and minimalistic user interfaces. We are interested in building partnerships with you to realize your ideas of internet application. We provide the technical capabilities and support to complement your domain specific expertise and entry point to customers. Our vision of open source protocols, standards and software, our focus on the user experience and our company culture will enable us to build applications that add true value to the user and leverage emerging technologies.
